Experience


From Sep. 2007 to Jun. 2011, I was pursuing my Bachelor degree in Xidian University, Xi'an, China. Network Engineering was my major back at that time. So, I learned relatively more knowledge about networking and engineering. It was fun to explore different technologies and get to know the details, therefore I decided to do my Master degree in Xidian University from 2011 to 2014.

From Jan. 2014 to Mar. 2014, I was at JAIST as a visiting student and researched on Network Security and Applied Cryptography. It was my honor to work with Prof. Atsuko Miyaji. The working environment in JAIST is really good and the people there are awesome!

After graduation from Xidian University, I joined CMB as an software engineer from Jul. 2014. The credit card department of CMB has a very open-minded leader team and people there are all friendly and active.

From Sep. 2014 till now, I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science. As mentioned above, I am currently an anomaly detection guy working on different applications and research for anomaly detection. Hopefully, I would graduate at summer 2018.
